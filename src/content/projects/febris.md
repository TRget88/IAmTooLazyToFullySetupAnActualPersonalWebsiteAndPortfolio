**The problem.** Enterprises want immersive XR and AR training, but the reality
on the ground is compliance rules, locked-down networks, and sites that are
secure or disconnected by design. Most training platforms assume a friendly cloud
and a steady connection. Real industrial and defense environments offer neither.

**What it is.** Febris is my company. I founded it in 2019 and built an enterprise
XR training marketplace end to end as the sole owner, from product vision through
architecture to enterprise deployment. I ran discovery across dozens of enterprise
organizations and turned their real constraints, the compliance and deployment
ones nobody likes to talk about, into a shipped product.

## How it works

Febris runs on .NET microservices on Azure Kubernetes Service, with offline-first
edge sync so training still works inside secure and disconnected environments. The
hard part was never the headset. It was making enterprise-grade delivery survive
contact with networks that trust nothing by default.

## Role

Founder and product lead, 2019 to present. Sole owner, from vision to enterprise
deployment.
